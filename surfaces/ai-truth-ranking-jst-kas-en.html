Status legend

  • DATA — the table is populated with real rows taken directly from the source spreadsheet.
  • GAP — a field/entry with no hard evidence in the spreadsheet; marked instead of being fabricated.
  • internal procedure in force — an adopted internal act (ordinance/rules) governing the use of AI.
  • pilot / pilot deployment — a test launch of a tool (e.g. PLLuM) confirmed by an announcement.
  • governance / competences — a team or policy appointed, without a full tool implementation.
  • strategy / concept — a directional document or report without a confirmed production implementation.
  • planned / guidelines — an announcement of an implementation or instructional material.
  • no hard evidence / to be verified — no public confirmation; a control entry.

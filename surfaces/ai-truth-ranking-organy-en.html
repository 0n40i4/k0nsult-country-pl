Status legend

  • DATA — the table is populated with real rows taken directly from the source spreadsheet.
  • GAP — a field/entry with no hard evidence in the spreadsheet; marked instead of being fabricated.
  • implementations / pilots — a tool launched or tested, confirmed by an announcement/document.
  • team / coordination / governance — a team, plenipotentiary or policy appointed, without a full tool implementation.
  • strategy / concept / analysis — a directional document, report or expert study without a production implementation.
  • no hard evidence / to be verified — no public confirmation; a control entry.
The claim≤proof principle. Entries with low readiness and a "no hard evidence / to be verified" status are not presented as implementations — they indicate the absence of public confirmation within the scope examined and require a freedom-of-information (public information access, DIP) request or a BIP query. We do not present them as facts.
